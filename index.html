AP Computer Science Principles

Abby Shanker


My name is Abby Shanker, and during my Junior year, I took AP Computer Science Principles. Last year, I took AP Computer Science A, which focused on the application of Java. I decided to take AP Computer Science Principles because I wanted to gain a better understanding of the foundation of computer science. I really liked the AP Computer Science Principles curriculum. Since I took the Java class before the principles class, it was difficult to combine learning a new programming language with learning the logic of computer science. After taking this course, I feel more prepared to take a rigorous computer science course. I gained an in-depth understanding of the principles that are fundamental to understanding new coding languages.


In this portfolio, I have included my Performance Task for the AP Computer Science Principles exam, a password generator, and a game design project. I have also included a reflection on my experience in this course.

Enjoy!
